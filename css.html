Specificity

CSS gives priority to the most specific selectors

From the most generic to the most specific:

  • Universal selector: *
  • Elements: body
  • Class: .button
  • ID: #comments
  • Inline styles: <p style="color: white"></p>
  • Important: color: red !important;

Specificity
